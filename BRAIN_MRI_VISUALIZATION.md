# Brain MRI Visualization - Complete Code

This is the complete brain visualization tab content that shows an MRI-style axial view of the brain with risk heat mapping.

The visualization includes:
- Realistic brain anatomy (axial/horizontal view)
- Left and right hemispheres
- Gray matter and white matter
- Hippocampus regions (highlighted with risk overlay)
- Ventricles
- Heat map overlay showing risk levels
- Anatomical orientation markers
- Clinical interpretation

The code has been implemented in RiskCalculator.tsx under the "brain" tab.
